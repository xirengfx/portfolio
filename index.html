Summary

Emanuel Borohov

Started using Photoshop about 10 years ago and I was really fascinated at the things I could do with this program, so I made it a hobby. In around 2019, I began taking graphic design more seriously. Recently I decided to deepen my knowledge with Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator and take courses to get a certification in order to develop a career as a graphic designer rather than just doing it as a hobby. Currently working as a freelancer.
